About Pizza Day

Pizza Day was inspired by the first Bitcoin transaction in history on May 22, 2010. On this day, two pizzas were purchased for 10,000 bitcoins, marking an important milestone in the world of cryptocurrencies and the birth of a meme.

Pizza Day goes beyond the simple meme. Our goal is to build the largest crypto community in the world, providing interactivity through our platform. Here, participants can engage in competitions, win prizes, and at the same time, learn more about the world of cryptocurrencies.

Additionally, Pizza Day offers delicious pizza recipes for you to try at home, and soon we will launch our DEX. In this exchange, users will be able to perform token swaps, staking, lending, liquidity pools, and much more.
